### Background

Campus Climate Network (CCN) is the leading hub of the climate justice movement on college campuses across America. CCN provides student organizations the resources, skills, and network to run winning campaigns on campuses.

After graduating from NYU, I wanted to keep supporting the student climate movement despite no longer being a student. CCN gave me an incredible opportunity to do just that. I joined as a Key Advisor in August 2023, mentoring students and advising chapters on their fossil fuel divestment campaigns — a role I still hold today.

In June 2025, I also stepped into the role of Communications Lead. I advise the Executive Director and Managing Director on organizational strategy, grants, and press. I lead CCN's design work, from the [website](https://www.campusclimatenetwork.org) to the annual impact reports that go out to funders.
